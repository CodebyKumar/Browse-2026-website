About Browse

Where Technology
Meets Innovation

BROWSE 2026 is a premier national-level technical symposium organized by the Associations of CSE, ISE, AI&DS and CSE(AI&ML) at Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumakuru.

We bring together the brightest minds in technology to showcase talent, exchange ideas, and explore cutting-edge innovations through a diverse range of challenging and exciting technical events.
